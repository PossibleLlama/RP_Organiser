RP Organiser

Aiming to allow easy input and retreival of information for role playing sessions.

Having a few different groups, long running or infrequent games, makes it difficult to keep track of what is happening, who has done what and who all these characters are.

With this you should be able to easily review previous sessions, characters and the worlds that they are a part of. The more information you add, the easier it is to find what you want, when you want it.
